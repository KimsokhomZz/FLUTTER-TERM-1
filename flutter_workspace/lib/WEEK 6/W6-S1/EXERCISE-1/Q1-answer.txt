Q1 - In what scenarios might one approach be more advantageous than another?
(in terms of readability, maintainability, performance, etc.)?

__ANSWER__: In terms of readability, maintainability, performance, etc..., One approach which more advantageous than another is
**Using a Dedicated Function** 
because this approach separate the logic of generating widgets into a dedicated function that improve readability, especially when
the widget creation logic is more complex.
